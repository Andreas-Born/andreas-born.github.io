Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate in economics at the Stockholm School of Economics. My research focuses on topics in behavioral economics and microeconomics. Recently I have been focussed economic promises in markets without binding contracts and selection of both genders into leadership positions. I am also interested to combine machine lerning techniques with causal inference.
In my research I have worked with experimental and non-experimental data, game theory, econometrics, natural language processing and machine learning.


I will graduate in summer 2019 and am currently on the job market.
