Welcome!

I am a Behavioral Economist and Data Scientist at Google who previously worked at Spotify. I hold a PhD in Economics from the Stockholm School of Economics. This website contains my academic research, and an overview over courses and talks I have given. My work focusses on experimentation, metric creation, model building, applied statistics and some economics. My research involved experimenation, causal inference, game theory, econometrics, and machine learning. If you like to get in contact feel encouraged to send me an email or message me on Linkedin.
