Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate in economics at the Stockholm School of Economics. My research focuses on behavioral economics and microeconomics. On this website you can find my research, an overview over my teaching, and my CV. Recently I have been particularly interested in economic promises in markets without binding contracts. I am also interested in methods such as natural language processing.


Currently, I am on the job market and will be available for interviews at the ASSA Meeting in Atlanta and the Winter Meeting of the Econometric Society in Naples.
