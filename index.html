Welcome!

I am a PhD Economist graduated from the Stockholm School of Economics. I currently work as a Data Scientist for research and developement in personalization at Spotify. This website presents my academic research with a focus on topics in behavioral economics and microeconomics. Recently I have worked on economic promises in markets without binding contracts and selection of both genders into leadership positions.
In my research I work with experimental and non-experimental data, game theory, econometrics, natural language processing and machine learning.
